Find the perfect spot
Can be installed on most walls Tonal is installed similarly to TV mount brackets and works with most wood and metal studs 16-24 inches apart.
Minimal space is required The ideal overhead height clearance is 7' 10" to allow Tonal's adjustable arms to fully extend. However, Tonal can still work with a ceiling height of 7'.
Stay connected Choose a space with continuous, reliable WiFi and a grounded, three-prong power outlet within 6 feet of the location without extension cords.
